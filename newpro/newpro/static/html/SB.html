SBI BANK

State Bank of India (SBI) is an Indian multinational public sector bank and financial services statutory body headquartered in Mumbai. SBI is the 49th largest bank in the world by total assets and ranked 221st in the Fortune Global 500 list of the world's biggest corporations of 2020.it is a public sector bank and the largest bank in India[7] with a 23% market share by assets and a 25% share of the total loan and deposit market.
